# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You have received the following email from your English pen friend, Anne:

> It's a really brave step to take a year out of college to come and spend 6 months in the UK!
> 
> What are you planning to do with your time?
> Find a job?
> Learn some new skills?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write back and give me more details of your plans so I can do my best to help you have a good time.
Write an email to Anne, responding to her questions and sharing your plans for your time in the UK.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
