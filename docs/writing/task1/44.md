Dear Andy,

Thank you for your letter. I understand your problem about the homework. I am happy to tell you about my favourite movie.

My favourite movie is Interstellar. It is a science fiction film. The director is Christopher Nolan. I watched it for the first time with my family at the cinema in 2014.

The story is about a group of astronauts who travel through space to find a new planet for humans to live on. They go through a special tunnel in space called a wormhole. It is also a story about a father and his daughter who love each other very much.

I love this movie because it is very exciting and emotional. The special effects are amazing and the music is very beautiful. By the end of the movie, I was almost crying.

I think this is a great movie for you to write about!

Cheers,
