# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You have just received a letter from a friend, Audrey, asking about your new puppy.

> It’s great to receive your letter. I’m sorry I haven’t written for a long time. I was busy preparing for final exams. I also try to help my parents with the housework since they are busy all day.
> 
> What a great news you have a new puppy! I really love puppies because they are cute.
> What’s the name of your puppy?
> Why have you decided to give it that name?
> Who gave you such a wonderful gift?
> Also, do you have any difficulties looking after it?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write a letter responding to Audrey.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
