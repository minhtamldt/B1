You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.
Read this extract from a letter you have recently sent to a friend:

Oh, and by the way, don't go to XYZ Restaurant for your birthday. We went there last night - the service was awful and the food was a disaster! I complained to the head waiter but he asked me to put it in writing.


Write a formal letter to the restaurant manager complaining about your experience and requesting appropriate action.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
