Dear Peter,

I am so happy to read your letter! Your trip on the sea sounds very interesting and exciting. I am glad you did not feel seasick!

Let me tell you about my last holiday. I went to Da Lat with my family for a long weekend. Da Lat is a beautiful city in the mountains.

The weather there was very cool and nice, not hot like in our city. We visited many beautiful flower gardens and local markets. I also ate a lot of fresh strawberries there. They were very sweet and delicious.

In the afternoon, we rented bicycles and rode through a pine forest. It was very peaceful and beautiful. I really enjoyed it.

After the holiday, I will go back to school and study hard for my exams.

I hope we can meet again soon!

Cheers,
