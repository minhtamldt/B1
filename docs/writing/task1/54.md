# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You had a party at a restaurant. You received an email from Mr. Watson, the manager of the restaurant, asking for your feedback. Read part of the letter from him below.

> Thank you for choosing to hold your party in my restaurant.
> 
> Can you give me some feedback about the food, the service, and the atmosphere? Is there anything you were unhappy with? Can you suggest how to improve my restaurant?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an email responding to him.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
