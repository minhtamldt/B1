Dear Mr. Watson,

Thank you for reaching out and for giving me the opportunity to share my feedback about my recent experience at your restaurant. Overall, it was a very pleasant evening, and our group had a wonderful time.

Starting with the food, the quality was genuinely impressive. Every dish arrived fresh and beautifully presented, and the flavours were exceptional. In particular, the grilled salmon and the signature dessert platter were absolute highlights — several of my guests are still talking about them!

In terms of service, the waiting staff were attentive and professional throughout, always on hand without ever making us feel rushed. We were especially grateful for the way the team handled our last-minute seating arrangement change with such grace and efficiency.

As for the atmosphere, the warm lighting and elegant décor created an intimate and sophisticated ambience that was perfectly suited to our celebration.

If I were to suggest one area for improvement, it would be to consider adding more vegetarian options to the main menu, as two of our guests found the current selection rather limited.

Once again, thank you for a wonderful evening. We will absolutely be returning!

Yours sincerely,

🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1**

Dear Mr. Watson,

Thank you for asking for my feedback about the party. I am happy to share my thoughts with you.

First, the food was very good. Everything tasted fresh and delicious. My favourite dish was the grilled salmon. Many of my friends also loved the desserts. They were very tasty!

Second, the service was great. The staff were very friendly and helpful. They always came quickly when we needed something. I was also impressed by the way they changed our table arrangement without any problem.

Third, the atmosphere was very nice. The lights were warm and the decorations were beautiful. It felt very elegant and comfortable for our celebration.

However, I have one small suggestion. Some of my friends do not eat meat, and they found it hard to find good vegetarian dishes on the menu. I think adding more vegetarian options would make the restaurant even better.

Thank you again for a great evening!

Yours sincerely,

*(161 Từ)*

**🇻🇳 Bản dịch (B1):** Kính gửi ông Watson, Cảm ơn ông vì đã hỏi thăm. Đồ ăn rất ngon và tươi. Món cá hồi yêu thích nhất. Dịch vụ rất tật — nhân viên thân thiện, phản xạ nhanh, giúp đổi chỗ ngồi không vấn đề. Không khí sang trọng và ấm cúng. Gợi ý: bổ sung thêm món nhà hàng cho người ăn chay vì lựa chọn hiện tại hơi ít.

**🧠 Mẹo (B1):** Trả lời đủ 3 ý: đồ ăn, dịch vụ, không khí. Thêm 1 gợi ý cải thiện. Tone phải lịch sự và chân thành. Dùng *"First, Second, Third, However"* là cấu trúc hoàn hảo cho dạng feedback.
