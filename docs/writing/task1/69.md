Dear John,

Thank you for your letter! I am very happy to hear that you are coming to visit my country. One week is a great time!

First, let me tell you how to get from the airport to my house. You can take bus number 86 from the airport. It will take you to My Dinh Bus Station. From there, you can take a taxi to my house. The trip takes about 45 minutes.

Second, there is a cheap guesthouse near my house called Hoa Viet Inn. It is only 5 minutes from my home and the price is about 250,000 VND per night. It is very clean and comfortable.

Third, I would love to take you on a street food tour! The food in my country is really amazing. I will take you to try pho, banh mi, and many other local dishes. I am sure you will love it!

I cannot wait to see you!

Big hugs,
