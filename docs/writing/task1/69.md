# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You have received a letter from your English-speaking friend, John, who is planning to come to your country for a one-week holiday. Read a part of his letter below.

> Hi! How are you doing? I've got one week free this summer and I'd really like to spend my time touring around your country.
> 
> How can I get to your house from the airport? Another thing, I will also need somewhere to stay - but not too expensive, of course. Everyone says that food in your country is the best, so if you have the time, would you take me on a street food tour or travel around with me? It would be great if you could.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write a letter responding to him.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
