🏆 **2. BÀI MẪU B2 - C1**

Dear Robert,

Thank you so much for thinking of me! You know me too well — Linkin Park is absolutely my all-time favourite band, and the thought of seeing them live in Hanoi is almost too good to be true.

To answer your questions: yes, unfortunately I will be going back to our hometown to celebrate our grandparents' golden wedding anniversary. Fifty years together is such a monumental milestone and there is no way I could miss it.

However, I was wondering if you could hold onto both tickets for now, as I would love to find a way to manage both events. If there is absolutely no way around the scheduling conflict, I would be happy to take one ticket and find a friend to take the other.

As for the price, I would be willing to pay up to 500,000 VND per ticket, which I believe is a fair price given how sought-after these tickets are. I could transfer the money directly to your bank account this weekend. As for receiving the ticket, perhaps we could arrange a quick meetup before you leave for the family event?

Thanks again for thinking of me. Fingers crossed!

Cheers,

🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1**

Dear Robert,

Thank you for your letter. I am very happy to hear that Linkin Park is coming to Hanoi. I love them too!

Sadly, I cannot go to the concert with you because I need to go back to our hometown for our grandparents' 50th wedding anniversary. I cannot miss this special day for our family.

But I still want to buy your tickets. I want to buy one ticket. I can pay 400,000 VND for it. I think that is a fair price.

For the payment, I can transfer money to your bank account this weekend. For receiving the ticket, can we meet before you go home? That would be the easiest way.

Thank you so much for thinking of me. I am very sorry I cannot go to the full show. I hope Linkin Park will come back to Vietnam again soon!

Cheers,

*(148 Từ)*

**🇻🇳 Bản dịch (B1):** Gửi Robert, Cảm ơn thư của bạn. Mình rất vui khi nghe Linkin Park đến Hà Nội. Mình cũng rất yêu họ! Tiếc là mình không thể đi vì phải về quê dự lễ kỷ niệm 50 năm ngày cưới của ông bà. Nhưng mình vẫn muốn mua 1 vé. Mình có thể trả 400,000 đồng. Mình sẽ chuyển khoản cuối tuần này. Để nhận vé, chúng ta có thể gặp nhau trước khi bạn về quê không? Cảm ơn và chúc bạn vui!

**🧠 Mẹo (B1):** Đề hỏi 4 ý: có muốn mua không, mua bao nhiêu vé, trả bao nhiêu tiền, nhận vé như thế nào. Hãy trả lời từng câu hỏi một cách ngắn gọn và rõ ràng.
