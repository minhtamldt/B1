Dear Carol,

Thank you for your email. I liked reading your letter a lot. I also have a busy week like you, so I understand how important free time is!

In my free time, I like doing both staying at home and going out. It depends on the weather and my mood.

When the weather is nice, I love going outside. Every Sunday morning, I go cycling with my best friend around a beautiful lake near my house. It is very fun and good for my health.

But on weekday evenings, I prefer staying at home. I like listening to music and drinking tea while reading a good book. I also enjoy drawing because it helps me relax after a long day at school.

I hope you can go out more often on weekends with your friends! Maybe you can talk to your parents about it.

Can't wait to hear back!

Best,
