Dear Bill,

I was so sorry to hear that you have been struggling with your health recently. I can imagine how tough things must be right now, and I want you to know that you have my full support.

First and foremost, I strongly urge you to consult a doctor as soon as possible. A medical professional can provide you with a personalised plan to quit smoking, which may include nicotine replacement therapy options such as patches or gum. These have been proven to significantly ease withdrawals and cravings.

In the meantime, there are a few practical steps you can take to get started. Every time you feel the urge to smoke, try distracting yourself by going for a short walk, drinking a glass of water, or simply calling a friend. Joining a local support group for people who are trying to quit would also be incredibly beneficial, as you would feel less alone in this journey.

Remember, quitting smoking is one of the best gifts you can give yourself. I truly believe in you, and I am here for you every step of the way. Don't hesitate to reach out whenever you need a chat.

Take care of yourself!

Best,

🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU TỐI THIỂU (MỤC TIÊU B1 - CHỐNG LIỆT, DỄ THUỘC)**

Dear Bill,

Thank you for sharing your problem with me. I am very sorry to hear about your health. I will try to give you some advice about how to quit smoking.

First, I think you should go to see a doctor. A doctor can help you find the best way to stop smoking. They can also give you medicine to help.

Second, you should try to keep yourself busy when you want to smoke. For example, you can go for a walk, drink some water, or call a friend. This will help you forget about cigarettes.

Finally, please remember that you are not alone. I will always be here to support you. Quitting smoking is hard, but I believe you can do it.

Please write back to let me know how you feel. I will always think about you.

Best,

**🧠 Mẹo ôn tập (B1):**
- **Khung sườn cảm hủ:** Mở bài bằng *"I am very sorry to hear about..."* để thể hiện sự đồng cảm. Kết bài bằng *"I will always be here to support you"* — câu này dễ học thuộc và rất ăn điểm Task Fulfillment.
- **Nêu lời khuyên bằng cấu trúc đơn giản:** Dùng *"I think you should..."* → *"For example..."* để giải thích lý do. Cấu trúc này dễ áp dụng cho mọi loại đề advice-giving.
