Dear Alex,

It is fantastic to hear from you again. I am thrilled to know that your friends are planning a backpacking trip around Vietnam. I would be more than happy to share some recommendations with them.

To explore Vietnamese culture and history deeply, they should definitely kick off their journey in Hanoi. The capital city is home to fascinating historical landmarks such as the Temple of Literature and the Old Quarter. From there, they can easily travel southwards. 

As for transportation, the easiest and most authentic way to get around is by using open tour buses or taking the train. These options are not only affordable but also allow them to enjoy breathtaking landscapes along the way. 

Since they are on a tight budget, I highly recommend staying in youth hostels or local homestays. These places are surprisingly cheap, clean, and provide a great opportunity to interact with incredibly hospitable locals. 

I hope this information is helpful. Let me know if you or your friends need any further details!

Thank you for your email. I am very happy to tell you about traveling in Vietnam. 

First, I think your friends should begin their trip in Hanoi. It is the capital of Vietnam and has a very interesting history. There are many old buildings and museums there.
