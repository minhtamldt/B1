You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.
You have asked your closest friend Brianna to help look after your house and pet when you are on holiday. She has written you an email asking for more information. Read this part of her email below.

Great to hear that you are going to visit Dubai! I'm sure you will have a wonderful time there. Don't worry, I'll look after your house and your pet while you are away. I just want to know the time you leave for Dubai and when you will be back. I don't have a lot of experience looking after animals, so tell me how to care for your pet. Also, are there any other household duties I am supposed to do?
Lots of love,
Brianna

Write a letter to respond to Brianna.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.

---

## TRẢ LỜI TỪ AGENT:

### Bước 1: Phân tích đề bài
*   **Dạng thư (Purpose):** Thư cung cấp thông tin và hướng dẫn.
*   **Người nhận thư (Recipient):** Brianna (bạn thân).
*   **Giọng điệu (Tone):** Thân mật (Informal).
*   **Thì (Tense):** Tương lai đơn / Hiện tại tiếp diễn mang ý nghĩa tương lai, Hiện tại đơn.
*   **Nội dung cốt lõi:**
    1.  Thời gian bạn khởi hành đi Dubai và lúc nào trở về.
    2.  Cách chăm sóc thú cưng.
    3.  Các công việc nhà (household duties) khác cần làm.

---

### Bước 2: Dàn ý (Outline)

*   **Lời chào:** Dear Brianna,
*   **Mở bài:** Cảm ơn Brianna đã nhận lời trông nhà và thú cưng giúp. Bày tỏ sự hào hứng về chuyến đi.
*   **Thân bài:**
    *   *Ý 1 (Lịch trình):* Nêu cụ thể ngày, giờ chuyến bay đi và ngày giờ trở về.
    *   *Ý 2 (Chăm sóc thú cưng):* Đưa ra hướng dẫn đơn giản.
    *   *Ý 3 (Việc nhà):* Liệt kê 1-2 việc nhà nhẹ nhàng.
*   **Kết thư:** Biết ơn sự giúp đỡ.
*   **Chào tạm biệt:** Lots of love, / Best,

### Bước 3: Bài mẫu xuất sắc (Trình độ B2-C1)

Dear Brianna,

Thanks a million for agreeing to house-sit and look after Max for me! It takes a huge weight off my mind so I can fully enjoy my trip to Dubai without worrying about things at home.

Regarding my itinerary, my flight departs next Monday at 8 AM, and I’ll be touching down back home on the following Sunday evening, around 7 PM. 

As for Max, please don't worry because he’s incredibly low-maintenance. You just need to feed him twice a day—once in the morning and once at 6 PM. His kibble is in the pantry, and please ensure his water bowl is always full. He’d also appreciate a short stroll around the block in the late afternoon to burn off some energy. 

In terms of household chores, there isn't much to fret about. Could you do me a favor and water the indoor plants every couple of days? Also, please check the mailbox and take the trash out on Thursday morning.

I really owe you one! I’ll definitely bring you back a lovely souvenir from the souks in Dubai. 

Lots of love,

*(188 words)*

**Từ vựng & Cấu trúc "ăn điểm" (Vocabulary & Highlighted Structures):**
*   **Thanks a million:** Cực kỳ cảm ơn (Informal).
*   **Take a huge weight off my mind:** Trút bỏ gánh nặng.
*   **Itinerary (/aɪˈtɪn.ə.rər.i/):** Lịch trình.
*   **Touch down:** Hạ cánh.
*   **Low-maintenance:** Dễ chăm sóc.
*   **Do me a favor:** Giúp tôi một việc.
*   **I owe you one:** Tớ nợ cậu một ân tình.

### Bước 4: Bài mẫu an toàn (Trình độ B1)

Dear Brianna,

Thank you so much for your help. I am very happy that you will look after my house and my pet when I travel to Dubai.

First, let me tell you about my schedule. I will leave for Dubai at 8 a.m. next Monday. I will come back home on Sunday evening next week. 

Second, my dog's name is Max. He is very friendly so you don't need to worry. You just need to feed him two times a day: in the morning and in the evening. His food is in the kitchen. Please give him some clean water too. 

Finally, about the household duties, you don't need to do much. Please help me water the plants in the living room every two days. Also, remember to take out the trash on Thursday night. 

Thank you again for your kindness. I will buy a special gift for you from Dubai.

Love,

*(158 words)*
