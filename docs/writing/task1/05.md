# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

This is an extract from your pen-friend's postcard:

> I'm having a busy time at the moment as I have to write lots of Christmas cards to all my friends and relatives.
> 
> Do you celebrate Christmas in Vietnam?
> What holidays are celebrated in your country?
> What is your favourite holiday?
> How do you celebrate it?
> 
> Well, I'd better go now. I've got to help mum with cooking.
> I can't wait to hear from you.
> Lots of love,
> Dan
> 
> 
> Write a letter responding to Dan. In your letter:
> - Answer his questions about Vietnamese holidays.
> - Ask 3 questions about British holidays.

**Yêu cầu:**
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
