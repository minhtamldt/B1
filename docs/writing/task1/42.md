# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You received a letter from a visitor, Sam, asking for some information about a house for rent.

> We’re going to visit London next month. I just saw your advertisement for flats today and I am writing to ask for more information. There’ll be 10 people in our group, so how many flats would we need to rent and how much will the total cost be for one week?
> 
> I’d like to know what the flats are equipped with and what kinds of facilities are available nearby. Are there any shopping centers? Finally, is it convenient to get to the city center from your place?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write a letter responding to him.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
