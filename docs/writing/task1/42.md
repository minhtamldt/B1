Dear Sam,

Thank you for your enquiry regarding our flats for rent in London. I am delighted to hear you are planning a visit and would be more than happy to assist you with all the information you need.

For a group of 10 people, I would recommend renting two of our spacious two-bedroom flats, which can comfortably accommodate 5 persons each. The total cost for one week would be £1,400, with each flat priced at £700 per week. All flats come fully equipped with modern furnishings, a fully fitted kitchen, high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen television, and central heating.

In terms of nearby amenities, you will be pleased to know that the area is exceptionally well-served. There is a large shopping centre just a 10-minute walk away, along with a supermarket, several restaurants, and a gym. As for accessibility to the city centre, our flats are conveniently located just a 5-minute walk from the underground station, so commuting around London will be an absolute breeze.

Please do not hesitate to get in touch if you require any further information. We look forward to welcoming you!

Yours sincerely,

🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1**

Dear Sam,

Thank you for your letter. I am happy to give you more information about the flats.

For a group of 10 people, you will need to rent two flats. Each flat has two bedrooms and can have 5 people. The price is £700 for one week for each flat. So the total cost for both flats is £1,400 for one week.

The flats have many things like a kitchen, a TV, Wi-Fi, and a heater. They are very comfortable and modern.

Near the flats, there is a big shopping center that is only 10 minutes away on foot. There is also a supermarket and some restaurants.

For the city center, it is very easy to go there. The underground station is only 5 minutes away from our flats.

I hope this information is helpful. Please write back if you have more questions.

Yours sincerely,

*(154 Từ)*

**🇻🇳 Bản dịch (B1):** Gửi Sam, Cảm ơn thư của bạn. Nhóm 10 người cần thuê 2 căn hộ, mỗi căn 5 người. Giá là £700/căn/tuần, tổng là £1,400. Căn hộ có bếp, TV, Wi-Fi và lò sưởi. Gần có trung tâm thương mại lớn chỉ 10 phút đi bộ và siêu thị. Muốn đi trung tâm thành phố chỉ cần đi bộ 5 phút tới ga tàu điện ngầm.

**🧠 Mẹo (B1):** Trả lời đủ 4 ý: số căn hộ, giá, trang thiết bị, khoảng cách tiện ích/trung tâm. Dùng câu đơn giản nhưng chính xác là đủ ăn điểm.
