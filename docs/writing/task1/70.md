Dear Jimmy,

Thank you for your letter! I am very excited to hear that you are coming to Vietnam next month. I cannot wait to see you!

First, I will tell you how to get from the airport to my house. You can take bus number 86 from the airport. Get off at My Dinh Bus Station. Then take a taxi from there. My house is only about 10 minutes from the station. The whole trip takes about 45 minutes.

Second, for a cheap hotel, there is a nice hotel near my house called the Green Star Hotel. It is only two streets away. The price is about 300,000 VND per night. It is clean and comfortable.

Third, for your father's coffee, I know a great place! There is a famous coffee shop called Trung Nguyen Legend in the city centre. They sell many kinds of delicious Vietnamese coffee. I will take you there!

I look forward to seeing you soon!

Lots of love,
