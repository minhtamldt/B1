🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1**

Dear Rose,

Thank you for your letter! I am happy to hear from you.

You asked me about my new job. I decided to change because my old job was very boring. Even though it paid well, I did not feel happy there. I like helping people and I have always been interested in education, so I applied for a new job.

Now I work as a content creator at an online education company. Every day, I create learning videos and materials for students. I work in a modern office in the city center and my working hours are quite flexible.

I am very happy with my new job because the work is interesting and meaningful. My new colleagues are also very friendly and hard-working. They are younger than my old colleagues, and the office has a very fun and creative atmosphere.

I hope you can visit me at my new office soon!

Warm wishes,
