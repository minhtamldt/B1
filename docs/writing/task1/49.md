Dear Andy,

Thank you for your email. I understand your problem. It is very hard to study for exams and keep doing the things you love at the same time.

But I think you should not stop your piano lessons. Your dream of playing the piano is very important. Also, I heard that playing music can actually help you study better because it trains your brain.

I think the best thing you can do is to make a timetable. Write down all the times for studying and also the time for piano practice. Try to follow the timetable every day. This way, you can do both things without missing anything.

Also, try not to waste time. When you study, focus and don't use your phone. Short and focused study sessions are better than long ones.

I hope things get easier for you soon. Good luck!

Best,
