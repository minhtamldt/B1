# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You’ve just received an email from Andy - an English pen friend, asking for some advice.

> Hi, how are you doing? I’m really enjoying my piano lessons, which I have in the evening every week. The teacher is so friendly and lovely. You know, playing the piano has always been one of my dreams since I was a child.
> 
> Unfortunately, I have a lot of homework and exams to prepare for at the moment. I need a lot of time to study, but I don’t want to stop going to the piano classes. What should I do now?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an email responding to him.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
