# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You received an email from Mr. Bernard Dener.

> I am organizing a trip for my students to visit your exhibition. I am writing to ask for details regarding the exact dates of the exhibition and daily opening hours.
> 
> I would also like to know the topic of the exhibition and the benefits students can get. As I mentioned, I am planning this tour for groups of students. I wonder if there are any discounts available.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write a letter responding to him.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
