🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1**

Dear Jim,

Thank you for your letter! A holiday in Switzerland sounds amazing. I am very excited for you!

Yes, I have finished my final exams. They were very difficult but I think I did OK. I am now very happy to have free time!

For this summer, I have some special plans. My family and I are planning a 10-day trip to some beautiful places in central Vietnam. First, we will visit Hue, which is an old city with many historical buildings. Then, we will go to Hoi An. Hoi An is very famous for its colourful lanterns and old streets. Finally, we will spend a few days relaxing on the beach in Da Nang.

I am so excited about this trip! I hope we can both share photos and stories about our holidays after we get back.

Lots of love,

**🇻🇳 Bản dịch (B1):** Gửi Jim, Cảm ơn thư! Chuyến Thụy Sĩ nghe thật tuyệt. Mình vừa thi xong rồi, rất vui được nghỉ! Kế hoạch hè là roadtrip 10 ngày ở miền Trung: Huế thành phố cổ, Hội An đèn lồng nổi tiếng và đi biển Đà Nẵng. Mình rất háo hức!
