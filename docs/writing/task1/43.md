Dear John,

I am sorry to hear about your eye problems. I think you should follow your doctor's advice and stop watching TV for so long.

For me, I watch TV for about 30 minutes a day. I know watching too much TV is bad for my eyes, so I try not to watch too much.

For relaxing, I have many other things I like to do. First, I love reading books. I am reading an interesting story now. Second, I like going for a walk in the evening around the park near my house. It helps me feel relaxed and healthy. I also enjoy cooking on weekends because it is fun and creative.

My favourite TV programs are nature documentaries. I like them because I can learn many new things about animals and the world.

I hope your eyes get better very soon!
