# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 1 (Thư / Email)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 20 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 120 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

You’ve just received an email from Lisa - an English pen friend, asking about learning English skills.

> How are you now? I’m going to take a language test in two months, and I’m now busy preparing for it. The most difficult skill for me is Listening. I find it really hard to understand what is being said.
> 
> I know that you got great results in your language test last month, so please give me some advice on how to improve my listening skills. Also, I always feel nervous before exams. Will you tell me what I should do to keep calm?

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an email responding to her.
You should write at least 120 words. Do not include your name. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
