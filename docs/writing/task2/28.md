# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 2 (Bài Luận / Essay)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 40 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 250 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

> Read the following text from an article about sports at school.
> 
> Sports have traditionally been a form of leisure, with a wide variety of games enjoyed across the world. However, in modern times, the role of sports in education has become a topic of debate. Some people believe that sports should be an essential part of school life for children, while others argue that it should be purely optional.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an essay for an educated audience discussing both perspectives on whether sports should be a mandatory part of school life or an optional activity. Support your answer with specific reasons and relevant examples. Give reasons and relevant examples from your knowledge or experience. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
