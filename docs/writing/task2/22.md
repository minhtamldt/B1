# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 2 (Bài Luận / Essay)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 40 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 250 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

> Read the following text from an article about the admission fee of museums.
> 
> A museum is an entity that preserves the artifacts of history and works as an important representative of a nation's tradition and culture. Thus, the importance of museums in conveying historical facts to the new generation is beyond controversy. However, the question of whether museums should charge an admission fee remains open to debate.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an essay for an educated audience discussing whether the advantages of charging an admission fee to museums outweigh the disadvantages. Support your answer with specific reasons and relevant examples. Give reasons and relevant examples from your knowledge or experience. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
