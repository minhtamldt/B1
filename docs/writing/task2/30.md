# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 2 (Bài Luận / Essay)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 40 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 250 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

> Read the following text from an article about zoos.
> 
> Living beings are an inseparable part of the natural world, and the treatment of animals remains a controversial issue. Some people feel that keeping animals in captivity, such as in zoos, is always wrong. Others argue that keeping animals in zoos benefits both animals and humans.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an essay for an educated audience discussing both perspectives on keeping animals in zoos. Support your answer with specific reasons and relevant examples. Give reasons and relevant examples from your knowledge or experience. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
