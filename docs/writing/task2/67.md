🏆 **2. BÀI MẪU C1 (~310 Từ)**

In the contemporary era, the traditional dynamics of the household have undergone a profound transformation, with women increasingly pursuing professional careers and achieving financial independence. Consequently, some argue that domestic skills such as cooking and cleaning are no longer essential for children, particularly girls. I strongly disagree with this assertion, as it conflates the necessity of manual labour with the vital importance of self-sufficiency and life management.

Firstly, the ability to cook and maintain a home is a fundamental pillar of personal independence, regardless of one's gender or professional status. While it is true that many successful career women can afford hired help or frequent restaurant meals, relying solely on external services is neither sustainable nor always advisable. Mastering basic culinary skills allows individuals to make informed, healthier choices about their nutrition, which is critical for long-term physical well-being. Furthermore, household management skills foster a sense of responsibility and discipline that translates directly into professional success. A child who understands how to organise their living space is better equipped to organise their time and projects in the workplace.

Secondly, the idea that these skills should be neglected, especially for girls, reinforces a false dichotomy between domesticity and professional ambition. Domestic skills should be viewed as "life skills" rather than gendered burdens. In the 21st century, both boys and girls should be encouraged to be versatile. A modern household thrives when every member can contribute to its upkeep. By exempting children from these chores, we risk raising a generation that is technically brilliant but practically helpless.

In conclusion, the rise of the career woman does not render domestic skills obsolete. On the contrary, in an increasingly fast-paced and outsourced world, the ability to care for oneself and one's environment remains a hallmark of a truly educated and capable individual. Both girls and boys should continue to learn these essential tasks as part of a well-rounded upbringing.

🛟 **3. BÀI MẪU B1 (~265 Từ)**

Nowadays, many women have successful careers and spend more time at work than at home. Because of this, some people believe that children, especially girls, do not need to learn how to cook or do housework. I disagree with this opinion. I think that every child needs to learn these skills for several reasons.

First of all, cooking and cleaning are essential life skills for everyone. Even if a person is very rich and has a housemaid, they should still know how to take care of themselves. For example, there might be times when the helper is not there, or when they live alone in a different city for work or study. Knowing how to cook a simple, healthy meal is much better for your health and budget than always eating at restaurants or ordering fast food.

Second, doing chores helps children become more responsible and independent. When children help with the housework, they learn how to manage their time and take care of their belongings. These are important lessons that will help them in the future. Moreover, I believe that housework should not just be for girls. Boys also need to learn how to cook and clean so that they can be helpful members of their families and societies.

In conclusion, I believe that the modern world does not change the fact that domestic skills are important. Parents should teach both their sons and daughters how to do household chores. These skills help them become healthy, independent, and successful adults in the future.

Kỹ năng nấu ăn và dọn dẹp là kỹ năng sống cần thiết cho tất cả mọi người. Dù giàu có, bạn vẫn cần biết tự chăm sóc bản thân khi ở một mình hoặc khi người giúp việc nghỉ. Ngoài ra, làm việc nhà giúp trẻ sống có trách nhiệm và độc lập hơn. Đặc biệt, cả con trai cũng nên học những việc này để giúp đỡ gia đình. Kết luận: Kỹ năng nội trợ giúp trẻ trở thành người lớn tự lập và thành công.
