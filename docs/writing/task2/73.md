# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 2 (Bài Luận / Essay)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 40 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 250 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

> Read the following text about transportation in cities.
>
> Traffic congestion is one of the most urgent problems facing modern cities around the world. Every year, traffic jams cost cities billions of dollars in lost productivity and cause serious environmental damage. While some see it as an unavoidable consequence of city growth, others argue that traffic jams have clear advantages as well.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an essay for an educated audience discussing the advantages and disadvantages of traffic jams for cities and their citizens. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.

---

## 💡 Gợi ý giải bài (Agent)

### Bước 1: Phân tích đề bài

- **Chủ đề (Topic):** Tắc đường (Traffic jams) nhìn từ hai chiều: lợi ích và tác hại.
- **Dạng bài (Type):** Advantages and Disadvantages (Lợi ích và Tác hại).
- **Nhiệm vụ (Task):**
  1. Phân tích những tác hại rõ ràng (mất thời gian, ô nhiễm, stress).
  2. Phân tích những lợi ích gián tiếp (thúc đẩy đầu tư hạ tầng, kinh tế địa phương).

### Bước 2: Dàn ý (Outline)

- **Mở bài (Introduction):**
  - Khái quát: Tắc đường là thực tế không thể tránh tại các đô thị phát triển.
  - Thesis statement: Bài viết phân tích cả ưu và nhược điểm của tình trạng này.

- **Thân bài (Body):**
  - **Đoạn 1 (Nhược điểm):**
    - Gây lãng phí thời gian, giảm năng suất lao động, tăng stress.
    - Xe cộ xả khí độc hại → ô nhiễm không khí, ảnh hưởng sức khỏe.
  - **Đoạn 2 (Ưu điểm gián tiếp):**
    - Áp lực tắc đường buộc chính phủ đầu tư vào hạ tầng giao thông dài hạn.
    - Kích thích kinh tế địa phương (quán ven đường, dịch vụ).

- **Kết bài (Conclusion):**
  - Dù có lợi ích gián tiếp, tác hại vẫn vượt trội; cần chính sách kiểm soát hiệu quả.

### Bước 3: Bài mẫu xuất sắc (Trình độ B2-C1)

Traffic congestion is widely regarded as one of the most pressing urban challenges today. As cities expand and vehicle ownership rises, gridlock has become unavoidable for millions of commuters. While the disadvantages are well-documented, a more nuanced analysis reveals certain indirect benefits worth considering. This essay examines both sides.

Undeniably, the drawbacks are significant. The most immediate consequence is the enormous loss of productive time. Workers trapped in daily gridlock arrive exhausted and delayed, reducing workplace productivity and eroding quality of life. From an environmental standpoint, idling vehicles release disproportionate quantities of carbon dioxide and toxic pollutants, worsening urban air quality and contributing to health problems such as respiratory and cardiovascular diseases.

Nevertheless, traffic congestion carries certain less obvious advantages. Severe gridlock often serves as a powerful catalyst for governmental investment in public infrastructure. When congestion reaches critical levels, authorities face public pressure to fund metro systems and highway expansions, bringing long-term benefits. Furthermore, heavy traffic inadvertently stimulates local commerce: roadside vendors and small restaurants along congested corridors experience a boost in customers, supporting local livelihoods. Additionally, high traffic volumes often reflect a thriving and dynamic urban economy.

In conclusion, while traffic congestion inflicts considerable costs on productivity, health, and the environment, it also acts as a driver of infrastructure development and local economic activity. The net balance, however, remains negative, and thoughtful urban policies remain essential.

*(248 words)*

**Từ vựng & Cấu trúc "ăn điểm":**
- **Pressing urban challenges:** Những thách thức đô thị cấp bách.
- **Idling vehicles:** Phương tiện đứng yên nổ máy.
- **Catalyst for investment:** Chất xúc tác cho đầu tư.
- **Inadvertently stimulates local commerce:** Vô tình kích thích thương mại địa phương.
- **Thriving urban economy:** Nền kinh tế đô thị đang phát triển mạnh.

### Bước 4: Bài mẫu an toàn (Trình độ B1)

Traffic jams are a common problem in many big cities today. Most people only see traffic jams as a bad thing, but there are actually some advantages as well. In this essay, I will discuss both the advantages and disadvantages.

On the one hand, traffic jams have serious disadvantages. The biggest problem is that people lose a lot of time. When workers spend one or two hours in traffic every day, they arrive at work late and tired, which makes them less productive and more stressed. In addition, traffic jams cause serious air pollution. When many cars and motorbikes sit still with their engines running, they release harmful smoke into the air. This damages the health of city residents and contributes to global warming.

On the other hand, traffic jams also have some positive effects. First, when traffic jams become very serious, the government is forced to spend money on better roads, new metro lines, and improved public transport. These improvements bring long-term benefits to the whole city. Second, traffic jams can help some local businesses. People who are stuck in traffic often stop to buy drinks or food from small shops on the roadside, helping local business owners earn more money.

In conclusion, traffic jams have clear and serious negative effects on people's health and time. However, they also push governments to invest in better infrastructure and support local businesses. Overall, the disadvantages are much greater, so authorities should still work hard to reduce traffic jams in cities.

*(259 words)*
