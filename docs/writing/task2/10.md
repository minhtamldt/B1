# 📝 Đề thi Writing Task 2 (Bài Luận / Essay)

> **⏱️ Thời gian làm bài:** Khoảng 40 phút
> **✍️ Độ dài yêu cầu:** Ít nhất 250 từ
> **🎯 Tiêu chí đánh giá:** Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary, Grammar

--- 

## 📄 Nội dung đề bài

> Read the following text from an article about the quality of life in large cities.
> 
> Recent years have witnessed the increasingly deteriorating quality of cities. This phenomenon is largely attributed to population explosion and pollution. Therefore, effective solutions must be proposed to tackle this issue.

**Yêu cầu:**
Write an essay for an educated audience discussing the causes and potential solutions to this problem. Support your answer with specific reasons and relevant examples. Give reasons and relevant examples from your knowledge or experience. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.
