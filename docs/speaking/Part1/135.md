# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about flowers.**
- Do you like flowers?
- What is your favorite flower?
- Do any flowers have a special meaning in your country?

**Now, let's talk about favorite places.**
- When you are sad, where do you often go?
- Do you prefer to be there alone or with someone else?
- Which places do you recommend your friends to go?
