# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about community service.**
- What kinds of community service have you done?
- Who usually joins in community activities?
- What are the benefits of participating in community service?

**Now, let's talk about your relationship with your parents.**
- Who are you closer to: your father or your mother?
- What do you like to do when you spend time together?
- Do you want to live with your parents when you get married?
