# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about going out.**
- How often do you go out? Why?
- Who do you usually go out with? Why?
- Where do you often go when you go out? Why?

**Now, let’s talk about a special place you would always want to return to.**
- Can you describe that place?
- Did you go there alone or with someone else?
- What makes you want to return to that place?
