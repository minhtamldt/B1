# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about restaurants.**
- Tell me about your favourite restaurant.
- What is your opinion of foreign food?
- Why do people like eating out?

**Now, let's talk about relaxation.**
- What activities can people do to relax?
- How do you usually relax?
- In what ways can you relax without spending any money?
