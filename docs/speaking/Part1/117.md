> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about banking in your area.**
- Have you ever been to a bank in your area?
- What did you do there?
- How does the ATM service work in your area?

**Now, let's talk about going out.**
- How often do you go out?
- Do you prefer going out alone or with friends? Why?
- What do you like to do when you go out?
