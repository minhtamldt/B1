# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about food.**
- What do you normally have for breakfast, lunch or dinner?
- How healthy is your diet?
- Where do you like to eat? Why?

**Now, let's talk about sports.**
- What do you do to stay fit?
- How important is sport to your family?
- What sports are you interested in? Why?
