# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about sports.**
- What kind of sports do you like?
- How often do you play sports?
- In your opinion, why do many people like playing sports?

**Now, let's talk about books.**
- What kind of books do you like?
- How many hours a day do you read books?
- What are the benefits of reading books?
