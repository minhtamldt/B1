# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about social networks.**
- What do you like about social networks?
- What social networks are you using now? Why?
- What is your advice for social network users?

**Now, let's talk about fashion.**
- How important is fashion to you?
- What kinds of clothes do you prefer to wear?
- Do you ever have to wear things you don't really like? If so, when and why?
