# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about your leisure time.**
- Do you enjoy your leisure time?
- What do you often do in your free time?
- Have your leisure activities changed since you were a child?

**Now, let's talk about books.**
- What kinds of books do you like to read?
- Do you read the same types of books now as you did 5 years ago?
- When do you think is the best time to read? Why?
