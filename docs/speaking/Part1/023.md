# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about museums.**
- Do you like visiting museums? If yes, how often? If not, why not?
- What is usually displayed in the museum near your home?
- How is visiting museums good or bad for young people?

**Now, let's talk about special events in your country.**
- What are some important events in your country?
- Can you name one or two things people commonly do during these events?
- Do you think it is a good idea to celebrate all such events in the same way?
