# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about habits.**
- What is your typical day like?
- Do you have any habits that annoy other people?
- What do you usually do when you feel upset?

**Now, let's talk about the weather.**
- What is the weather like today?
- Do you prefer hot or cold weather? Why?
- What activities do you recommend for different types of weather?
