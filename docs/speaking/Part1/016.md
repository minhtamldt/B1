# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about your reading habits.**
- Do you enjoy reading? Why or why not?
- What types of books or magazines do you like reading?
- Where do you prefer to read?

**Now, let's talk about your free time.**
- What do you like doing in your free time?
- Who do you enjoy spending your free time with?
- Where do you prefer to spend your free time?
