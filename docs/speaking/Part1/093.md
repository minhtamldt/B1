## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about renting a house or flat.**
- Have you ever rented a house?
- Would you prefer to pay the rent monthly or yearly?
- What do you look for in a rented house? Why?

**Now, let's talk about mobile phone service.**
- How often do you use your mobile phone?
- Are you satisfied with your mobile phone service? Why?
- In your opinion, what makes a good mobile phone service?
