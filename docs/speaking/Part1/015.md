# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about the rooms in your house.**
- Which room in your house do you like the most?
- What do you like doing there?
- Do you like having guests in your room? Why or why not?

**Now, let's talk about sports.**
- Do you play any sports?
- What sports do you enjoy watching?
- What sports do you enjoy playing?
