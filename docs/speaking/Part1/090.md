# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about fast food restaurants.**
- Have you ever been to a fast food restaurant?
- What is your favorite fast food?
- Is it healthy to eat fast food? Why or why not?

**Now, let's talk about sleeping habits.**
- How many hours do you sleep a day?
- How important is it to have a good sleep?
- What should we do to sleep better?
