# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about secondary school.**
- How do you feel about your secondary school?
- Which subject do you think is the most difficult for you?
- Are you still in contact with your friends from secondary school?

**Now, let's talk about lifestyle.**
- Do you have three meals a day?
- What do you often do to relax in the evening?
- Do you feel happy with your lifestyle?
