# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about pets.**
- Do you like animals?
- What is the most popular pet in your country?
- Why do people have pets?

**Now, let's talk about childhood.**
- What is your favorite childhood memory?
- Why did you like it?
- Do you still like it when you get older? Why?
