## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about freetime activities.**
- What do you often do in your free time?
- Which channel do you like most?
- What kind of books do you like?

**Now, let's talk about traffic in your town.**
- Is there much traffic in your town?
- What is the most common means of transport?
- What do you think are the solutions to traffic jams?
