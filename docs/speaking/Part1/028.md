> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about housework.**
- How do you share housework in your family?
- Do you think children should help with the housework?
- What are some activities that your family usually does together?

**Now, let's talk about fashion.**
- Do you try to follow the latest fashion trends?
- What kinds of clothes do you like to wear?
- Do you prefer shopping in small stores or supermarkets?
