# 🗣️ Đề thi Speaking Part 1 (Social Interaction)

> **⏱️ Thời gian dự kiến:** 3 phút
> **🎯 Tiêu chí:** Fluency, Lexical Resource, Grammar, Pronunciation

--- 

## 📄 Câu hỏi Giao tiếp xã hội

**Let's talk about swimming.**
- Do you like swimming?
- Do you prefer to swim alone or with someone else?
- Is it important to swim? Why or why not?

**Now, let's talk about high school.**
- What is your high school like?
- What happened on the first day of high school?
- Which high school subject do you think is most useful for people in adult life?
