Situation: Some of your friends are visiting you this weekend and you want to do something fun together. Three options are suggested: cook a meal together at your place, go to a local restaurant, or take them out for sightseeing. Which activity would you choose? Why?

**Candidate (Agent):**
**[Phiên bản 1] Thí sinh xuất sắc (C1):**
"Well, considering the three engaging options for hosting my visiting friends this weekend, I firmly believe that taking them out for sightseeing would be the most spectacular and memorable choice. Specifically, since they are visiting my locality, organizing a well-thought-out tour to explore our historic landmarks, iconic scenery, and vibrant local culture provides them with a uniquely immersive, authentic experience they simply couldn't replicate elsewhere. Furthermore, sightseeing naturally encourages dynamic interactions, walking, and taking wonderful photos, which serve as perfect souvenirs of our reunion. I definitely wouldn't opt for going to a local restaurant because, while the food might be exquisite, dining out is a relatively brief activity that lacks an enduring sense of adventure or novelty. As for cooking a meal together at my place, although it fosters intimacy, it could inadvertently become a massive chore requiring stressful grocery shopping and exhaustive cleaning up afterward, severely cutting into our quality bonding time. Therefore, an exciting sightseeing excursion is unequivocally my definitive decision."
