**Candidate (Agent):**
**[Phiên bản 1] Thí sinh xuất sắc (C1):**
"Well, considering the three gift options for my parents' milestone wedding anniversary, I unequivocally believe that a 3-day trip to the beach would be the most extraordinary and deeply appreciated present. First and foremost, a meticulously planned romantic getaway provides them with a rare, invaluable opportunity to escape their monotonous daily routines, rejuvenate their spirits, and rekindle their romance in a serene, breathtaking coastal setting. Furthermore, the precious memories and shared experiences they cultivate during this trip will undoubtedly outlast any material possession. I definitely wouldn't opt for a wedding cake, because despite its symbolic sweetness, it offers only fleeting enjoyment and is ultimately consumed and forgotten within hours. As for a family photo album, although it holds profound sentimental value and nostalgia, it lacks the active engagement and refreshing spontaneity that a restorative vacation provides. Therefore, sponsoring a memorable beach trip is absolutely my ultimate choice."
