Furthermore, it serves as a long-term educational investment that directly contributes to my future career prospects. I certainly wouldn't opt for a fashionable smartphone because while it might offer temporary gratification and social status, it is prone to causing unnecessary distractions from my studies. Therefore, purchasing a laptop is undeniably my ultimate decision."
