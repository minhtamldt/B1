Situation: Your town plans to organize an Open Festival and many visitors will attend. Three roles are suggested: a tour guide, a traffic instructor, and a master of ceremonies. Which role do you think would suit you best? Why?

**Candidate (Agent):**
**[Phiên bản 1] Thí sinh xuất sắc (C1):**
"Well, assessing the three volunteer roles for our town's upcoming Open Festival, I confidently assert that becoming a tour guide would be the absolute perfect fit for my skill set and personality. Primarily, I possess an outgoing demeanor paired with an extensive, in-depth knowledge of our local history, cultural heritage, and hidden culinary gems, allowing me to provide visitors with a truly immersive and memorable experience. Furthermore, walking around and interacting intimately with diverse international tourists would significantly enhance my cross-cultural communication skills. I certainly wouldn't choose to be a traffic instructor because standing at intersecting roads for hours under potentially harsh weather conditions is excessively physically exhausting, and I frankly lack the authoritative presence required to manage heavy congestion. Regarding the master of ceremonies role, while standing on stage is glamorous, the immense psychological pressure of addressing a massive crowd and improvising seamlessly in front of thousands is completely outside my comfort zone. Therefore, the tour guide position is unquestionably my definitive choice."

Do đó, vị trí hướng dẫn viên du lịch chắc chắn là sự lựa chọn dứt khoát của tôi.)*
