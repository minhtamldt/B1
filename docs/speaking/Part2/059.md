Situation: Your local council wants to make your area more environmentally friendly. Decide which method would be the most effective. There are THREE options for you to choose: putting recycle bins in public places, planting trees, and educating people about the environment. Which one would you choose? Why?

**Candidate (Agent):**
**[Phiên bản 1] Thí sinh xuất sắc (C1):**
"Well, scrutinizing the three proposed initiatives to enhance our area's ecological footprint, I passionately believe that profoundly educating people about the environment would undeniably be the most transformative and sustainable choice. Implementing comprehensive public awareness campaigns directly attacks the foundational root cause of ecological degradation by fundamentally shifting citizens' long-term mindsets and daily consumption habits, ensuring voluntary and persistent environmental stewardship. Merely putting out more recycle bins in public spaces is tragically ineffective if an uneducated populace lazily mixes their trash, which severely contaminates the recycling stream and defeats the entire purpose. While planting trees is a brilliant, undeniable aesthetic initiative for improving local air quality, it remains a purely passive measure that utterly fails to actively prevent residents from continuously littering or wasting vital resources. Therefore, empowering the community through education is irrefutably my ultimate strategy."
