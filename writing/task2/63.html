63

You should spend about 40 minutes on this task. Read the following text about art and culture.

Art is regarded as an important part of the culture of every country in the world. While some people think that art continues to inspire, more and more children seem to have less interest in art. Do you agree or disagree?

Write an essay to an educated reader discussing your opinion. Give reasons and relevant examples from your knowledge or experience. You should write at least 250 words. Your response will be evaluated in terms of Task Fulfillment, Organization, Vocabulary and Grammar.

VSTEP Exam Solver Result

🎯 1. XÁC ĐỊNH DẠNG ĐỀ BÀI (Type Identifier)

  • Phân loại: VSTEP Writing Task 2 (Essay).
  • Dạng đề: Opinion Essay — Do you agree or disagree? (Quan điểm).
  • Chủ đề: Trẻ em ngày càng ít quan tâm đến nghệ thuật — Bạn có đồng ý không?

🏆 2. BÀI MẪU C1 (~305 Từ)

The observation that contemporary children are exhibiting a diminishing interest in the arts, despite its historically celebrated role in cultural heritage, is a multifaceted issue that warrants serious examination. While I largely agree that traditional forms of art appreciation are facing a decline among the youth, I believe this shift is a reflection of evolving digital landscapes rather than a total loss of creative inclination.

One primary reason for this apparent disinterest is the pervasive influence of digital entertainment. In the modern era, children are inundated with highly stimulating, fast-paced content from video games, social media, and streaming platforms. Compared to the instant gratification provided by these digital mediums, traditional art forms like painting, sculpture, or classical music can seem static and demanding of a level of patience that many children have not cultivated. Consequently, the slow, contemplative process of creating or viewing art often struggles to compete for a child's limited attention span.

Furthermore, the increasing pressure for academic and professional success in STEM-focused economies has led to a marginalisation of art in school curricula. Parents and educators often prioritise mathematics, science, and technology, viewing art as a recreational "extra" rather than a core discipline. When art is treated as optional or undervalued by authority figures, children naturally mirror this attitude, viewing artistic pursuits as less important for their future.

However, it is worth noting that "art" itself is being redefined by the younger generation. Many children who show little interest in traditional gallery art are deeply engaged in digital illustration, video editing, and graphic design. While their tools have changed, their fundamental desire for creative expression remains.

In conclusion, I agree that interest in traditional art is declining due to digital distractions and academic pressures. However, we must recognise that children’s creativity is often being redirected into new, technological forms of artistic expression.

(302 Từ)

🇻🇳 Bản dịch (C1): Quan điểm trẻ em ít quan tâm đến nghệ thuật là đúng một phần. Lý do chính là sự thống trị của giải trí kỹ thuật số (game, mạng xã hội) mang lại sự phấn khích tức thì, khiến các môn nghệ thuật truyền thống trông có vẻ nhàm chán. Thêm vào đó, áp lực học các môn tự nhiên (STEM) khiến nghệ thuật bị coi nhẹ trong trường học. Tuy nhiên, trẻ em đang chuyển hướng sang nghệ thuật số như đồ họa, biên tập video. Vậy nên, sở thích không mất đi mà chỉ thay đổi hình thức.

🧠 Giải thích (C1): "diminishing interest" (sự quan tâm giảm dần), "pervasive influence" (ảnh hưởng sâu rộng), "instant gratification" (sự thỏa mãn tức thì), "marginalisation" (sự xem nhẹ/đưa ra ngoài lề), "STEM-focused" (tập trung vào Khoa học, Kỹ thuật, Kỹ sư, Toán).

🛟 3. BÀI MẪU B1 (~258 Từ)

Art is an important part of every country's culture. However, many people notice that children today seem to have less interest in art than in the past. In my opinion, I agree with this statement, and there are several reasons for this trend.

First of all, children today spend a lot of time on technology. Instead of drawing, painting, or visiting museums, many children prefer playing video games or watching videos on YouTube and TikTok. These digital activities are very exciting and easy to do, so traditional art can seem boring to them. Because they spend so much time on screens, they do not have much time left for artistic hobbies.

Second, the educational system and parents often focus more on "useful" subjects like math, science, and English. In many schools, art classes are very short or not considered important for the future. Parents also encourage their children to study hard to get a good job, and they often think that art is just a waste of time. When children see that adults do not value art, they stop being interested in it too.

However, I believe that art is still useful for children. It helps them develop their imagination and creativity. Even if they are not interested in traditional painting, many children still like creative things like taking photos on their phones or designing things on computer apps.

In conclusion, I agree that children have less interest in traditional art because of technology and school pressure. However, it is important for parents and teachers to encourage art because it is a vital part of a child's development and our culture.

(254 Từ)

🇻🇳 Bản dịch (B1): Nghệ thuật rất quan trọng nhưng trẻ em ngày nay ít quan tâm hơn. Lý do thứ nhất là vì các em dành quá nhiều thời gian cho công nghệ (game, mạng xã hội) thay vì vẽ hay đi bảo tàng. Lý do thứ hai là trường học và cha mẹ quá tập trung vào các môn "có ích" như Toán, Lý, Anh và coi nghệ thuật là lãng phí thời gian. Tuy nhiên, nghệ thuật vẫn cần thiết cho sự sáng tạo. Kết luận: Trẻ em ít quan tâm nghệ thuật truyền thống do áp lực học hành và công nghệ.

🧠 Mẹo (B1): Chia bài thành 2 đoạn thân bài rõ rệt: một đoạn về nguyên nhân công nghệ, một đoạn về nguyên nhân giáo dục. Luôn sử dụng câu kết luận để tóm tắt lại ý chính của mình.
